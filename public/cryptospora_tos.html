Terms of Service Page




     This page is meant to outline indemnities and protections for the pod maintainer and users in terms of legal considerations for any / all content stored on Cryptospora.net server/s. Cryptospora.net is a Diaspora pod, and as such, we run the back-end Diaspora peer-to-peer social networking suite based on a Ruby on Rails server installation. The backend code for Diaspora is accessible for verification and third-party review from our homepage, and also from the Diaspora github repository. Copyrighted Content      If Cryptospora.net is contacted by a content copyright holder about a violation of terms of their works in the content hosted on Cryptospora, our admins will first verify that the content is hosted on our server and that it does indeed violate US copyright law, then remove it.
